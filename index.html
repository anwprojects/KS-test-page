At Kumud Sampann, we believe the foundation of any great business is care

Care for people, for quality, and for growth. We are more than just a company. We are a family of brands that bring health, comfort, and inspiration into everyday life.

Whether it’s through Sailbrew, our fruit juices made with your well-being in mind, NEO, where we craft spaces that feel like home, or NEO Mentor, where we offer free education and personal mentorship to future designers, everything we do is guided by a deep sense of purpose and responsibility.

We invite you to explore everything we’ve created together. Each part of Kumud Sampann holds the spirit of family and the promise of something meaningful.

Neo Mentor is where creativity meets guidance. Founded by Kinjal Shah, our platform is designed for aspiring interior designers who want to learn the art of design through simplified lessons, project breakdowns, and real-world insights.

Kinjal Shah brings years of on-site, hands-on experience from real design and construction projects. Her deep industry knowledge shapes every lesson, making learning relatable, practical, and rooted in reality.

What We Offer:

  • Free learning via our YouTube channel
  • Personalized coaching and offline classes
  • Tips, tools, and tricks from a working professional

Why Learn with Us?

  • Beginner-friendly approach
  • Practical, project-based learning
  • Mentorship from an experienced designer with on-site industry exposure

Bottled Brilliance in Every Sip

From zesty Lemon Masala to refreshing Pink Peru and bold Nimbu Ginger, Sailbrew reimagines fruit juice for every mood. Available in convenient 200ml bottles and larger 1L and 5L gallon packs, we offer a colorful palette of flavors including Kiwi, Mojito, Jeera Masala, Blueberry, Strawberry, and more.

Our 1-liter and 5-liter gallon packs are perfect for family get-togethers, festive occasions, and everyday enjoyment. They also serve as a delicious base for homemade mocktails, fruit desserts, and creative kitchen experiments. Whether it's a small break or a big celebration, Sailbrew brings a healthy, flavorful touch to every moment.

Why Sailbrew?

  • Made with real fruit extracts.
  • Perfect for on-the-go refreshment or large gatherings.
  • Balanced sweetness with Indian taste notes.
  • Ideal for family-friendly recipes, mocktails, and refreshing desserts.
